Bridge Between

There is a lot of talk about the "bridge" between the physical world and the world of NFTs. This bridge is often thought of as a way to bring the benefits of blockchain technology to the physical world. In the simplest terms, the bridge between the physical world and NFTs allows for the tracking and exchange of physical assets on a blockchain. This can be anything from a piece of art to a car to a piece of land.

Vision & Value

NFT's provide a new way to think about value and ownership. They offer a way to create unique digital assets that can be owned and traded by anyone, anywhere. This new way of thinking about value opens up a world of possibilities for artists, creators, and collectors.

Physical World

In the physical world, we are constantly bombarded with stimuli. Our eyes take in visuals, our ears take in sounds, and our skin feels textures. Our brains are constantly processing this information, and it can be overwhelming. That's where NFT comes in. NFT is a way of filtering the physical world. It allows us to focus on one thing at a time and tune out the rest.

Community

07

Holding NFT

If you’re like most people, you probably think of crypto as digital money. But there’s a lot more to the world of cryptocurrency than just Bitcoin and altcoins. In fact, there’s a whole new world of digital assets known as non-fungible tokens, or NFTs. NFTs are digital assets that are unique and non-interchangeable. Unlike Bitcoin, which is fungible (meaning each Bitcoin is worth the same).

04

Metaverse

A metaverse is a user-created virtual world that exists on a blockchain. Metaverses are decentralized and often open-source, meaning anyone can create their own world or add to an existing one. NFT's will play a vital role in its expansion.

FAQ

What is NFT?

An NFT is a non-fungible token, which means it cannot be exchanged for another token of the same type. NFTs are often used to represent items such as digital art, collectibles, and in-game items.

What is MetaVerse?

A metaverse is a network of 3D virtual worlds focused on social connection. In futurism and science fiction, it is often described as a hypothetical iteration of the Internet as a single, universal virtual world that is facilitated by the use of virtual and augmented reality headsets.
